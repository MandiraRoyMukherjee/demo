For all purposes, to make service easy to provide, Technology plays a great role. Technology is the skills, methods, and processes used to achieve goals. It’s no secret that technology has become an integral component of daily life. In particular, it’s become rare to see children and teens without a digital device in hand wherever we go: restaurants, waiting rooms, parks and more.

Just like a coin has two sides Technology also has two sides one is positive that refer to the advantage of it's whereas, another is negative that refer to the disadvantage of it. 

The positive and negative effects of technology on children, Here are a few positive and negative effects technology can have on children to consider:

Positive:

Enhances Learning

Problem-Solving Skills

Analytical Skill

Develops Future Technological Leaders

Negative:

Diminishes Relationships and Social Skills

Less time for play and loss of social skills

So to conclude,
As the coin has both sides, even technology has both advantages and disadvantages. It is on parents for kids to stop use of excess and try to put their energy on building their future using technologies.
