About Us


Nov 2019: Humble begning of Fundo Kids as a indoor playzone in Raipur, C.G.
Jan 2020: Received overwhelming response from parents
Mar 2020: Introduced our learning programs for students of all age, every students can find suitable course for their needs.
Apr 2021: Set back as due to lockdown, had to close indoor playzone.
Jun 2020: Introduced robotics and IoT courses.
Nov 2020: More than 25 students got training on various programs.
Mar 2021: Created a community to promote arts & crafts among students
June 2021: Started weekend workshops for students on technical and non technical topics which can be useful for them. Again received overwhelming response by our belowed students and parents.
We pledge to continue our work and change the learning system.
We pledge to reach to every students with their suitable programs.
We pledge to make courses affordable to all.
We pledge to change the way of learning, make it more interactive, practical and fun way of learning.
